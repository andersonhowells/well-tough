Cookie policy

Cookies and tracking.

This website is intentionally simple. We do not currently use analytics cookies, advertising cookies or tracking pixels on the public pages.

Current position

The public Well Tough website does not currently set optional analytics or marketing cookies. Because of that, we do not show a cookie consent banner at this stage.

Third-party services

The contact form uses FormSubmit to deliver enquiries by email. If you submit the form, FormSubmit processes that submission as part of providing the form delivery service.

If this changes

If we later add Google Analytics, advertising pixels, embedded media or other non-essential tracking, we will update this policy and add an appropriate consent mechanism before those technologies run.
