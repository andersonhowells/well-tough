Privacy notice

How we handle your information.

This notice explains how Well Tough handles personal information submitted through this website, including contact enquiries and AI adoption assessment follow-ups.

Who we are

Well Tough is an AI adoption advisory based in Cobham, Surrey. You can contact us at info@welltough.co.uk or on 07958 412147.

What we collect

If you contact us, we may collect your name, work email address, phone number, company name, employee range, enquiry details and any information you choose to include in your message.

If you use the self-assessment, the results are calculated in your browser. We do not receive those answers unless you choose to share them with us through the contact form or a follow-up conversation.

Why we use it

We use enquiry information to respond to you, understand whether our services are relevant, prepare for diagnostic conversations, and keep a record of business development discussions.

Form provider

The contact form is processed by FormSubmit so that website enquiries can be emailed to us. FormSubmit may process the form submission as part of delivering that service.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry information only for as long as it is useful for responding to the enquiry, managing a potential client relationship, meeting legal obligations, or maintaining reasonable business records.

Your choices

You can ask us to correct, delete or stop using your personal information where applicable. To do that, contact info@welltough.co.uk.
